About Me

Education, Goals, etc.

I am a Game Programmer and graduate (Dec 2017) of Florida Interactive Entertainment Academy. I am interested in VR, RPG, MMOs, and FPS. I am currently trying to teach my self Python, MySql, HTML, CSS, & Javascript while maintaining my Unity/Unreal Engine skills.
